📖 THE SAGA OF BIG FELIZ 📖

Scroll down to read the official, unabridged history of your favorite teacher!

Chapter 1: The Early Bites (1960s - 1980s)

Born with a silver spoon and an iron stomach, Big Feliz discovered her two greatest passions early in life: World History and Deep Frying. Growing up, she spent her summers at the local library and her weekends at the county fair. In 1988, she achieved local legendary status when she won the Tri-State Hot Dog Eating Contest while simultaneously reciting the Magna Carta entirely from memory. It was on that fateful, mustard-stained day that she realized her destiny: to educate the youth.

Chapter 2: The Chalkboard Years (1990 - 2020)

For 30 glorious years, Feliz ruled Room 402 at Bulldog High. She pioneered the controversial but highly effective "Edible Education" method. Need to learn about the fall of the Roman Empire? Feliz demonstrated it by collapsing a giant, multi-layered lasagna onto a map of Europe. Physics? She taught trajectory using tater tots. She earned the prestigious Teacher of the Year award in 1998 and 2004, forming an unbreakable alliance with her best pals, Mr. Davis (Science) and Coach Beef (Gym).

Chapter 3: The Cyber-Renaissance (Present Day)

Retirement couldn't slow her down. Upon hanging up her chalkboard erasers, Feliz discovered a bold new frontier: The Information Superhighway! Trading her grading pen for an HTML editor and a 480p webcam, she now broadcasts her legendary Mukbangs directly from Biggie's Burgers. By combining her competitive eating background with her love for the World Wide Web, Big Feliz proves that learning never stops... and neither does the appetite!
