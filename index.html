Chapter 1: The Early Bites (1960s - 1980s)

Born with a silver spoon and an iron stomach, Big Feliz discovered her two greatest passions early in life: World History and Deep Frying. Growing up, she spent her summers at the local library and her weekends at the county fair. In 1988, she achieved local legendary status when she won the Tri-State Hot Dog Eating Contest while simultaneously reciting the Magna Carta entirely from memory.

Chapter 2: The Chalkboard Years (1990 - 2020)

For 30 glorious years, Feliz ruled ruled the 8th Grade at Saint Brigid School. She pioneered the controversial but highly effective "Edible Education" method. Need to learn about the fall of the Roman Empire? Feliz demonstrated it by collapsing a giant, multi-layered lasagna onto a map of Europe. She earned the prestigious Teacher of the Year award in 1998 and 2004.

Chapter 3: The Cyber-Renaissance (Present Day)

Retirement couldn't slow her down. Upon hanging up her chalkboard erasers, Feliz discovered a bold new frontier: The Information Superhighway! Trading her grading pen for an HTML editor, she now broadcasts her legendary Mukbangs directly from Biggie's Burgers.
